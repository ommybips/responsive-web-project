New Jeans

The group ushering a new generation of pop music.

Here's a time line of New Jeans:

  • 2011–2022 : Pre-debut activities and formation

    • Members of NewJeans had prior TV and music engagements, including Danielle Marsh and Hyein's involvement with U.SSO Girl and Play With Me Club, as well as Hanni Pham featuring in BTS's "Permission to Dance" video, with the group's formation starting in 2019 and finalizing its lineup under Hybe's ADOR label in March 2022, after being postponed from 2021 due to the pandemic.


  • 2022–present : Introduction, debut and Get Up

    • On July 22, 2022, ADOR surprised with the release of their debut single "Attention" and animated teasers, followed by the announcement of their debut EP containing four tracks, including the successful singles "Hype Boy" and "Hurt", leading to over 444,000 pre-orders in three days, as their unique promotional strategy garnered attention and accolades.

    NewJeans, a K-pop girl group, gained rapid success, releasing multiple hit singles and albums, including their debut EP with the standout track "Cookie," achieving million-selling records, winning awards, and collaborating with brands like Coca-Cola, while also making significant international appearances and performances like Lollapalooza and Summer Sonic Festival.
